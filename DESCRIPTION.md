## Description of the business

- We specialize in ultra high end audio solutions for the home that that seamlessly blend into a home's aesthetic
- Create spaces that offer a truly immersive audio experiences, including custom build speakers, amplifiers, and control systems
- We integrate lighting and tactile feedback (such as bass shaker floors) for the ultimate entertainment experiences that disappear into the background of the home
- We aim to provide systems with the power of professional commercial installations while maintaining the ease of use of a consumer product
- Provide powerful home automation systems that prioritize 100% reliability and complete offline control
- Offer AI home assistant systems that prioritize privacy and security and are fully open source and self hosted
- We are a full service company, from design to installation
- We are a small business, with a focus on personalized service
- Service area: Las Vegas and surrounding areas
- Contact: info@lossless.space
